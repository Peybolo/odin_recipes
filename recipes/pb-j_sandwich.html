PB&J Sandwich

Description

PB&J is a popular sandwich in the US, it consist of peanut butter and jelly between two pieces of sliced bread .

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Sliced bread

Steps

  1. Put the two pieces of sliced bread one next to each other.
  2. Put peanut butter in one of them and jelly on the other one
  3. Close the sliced bread making the peanut butter touch the jelly
  4. You're done!
