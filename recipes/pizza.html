Description

Napoletan Pizza is a typical italian dish. It is conformed with wheat-based thin dough, topped with tomato sauce, cheese (burrata) and oregano.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • Oregano
  • Cheese (Burrata)

Steps

  1. Put the pre-made pizza dough on a flat surface, extend it.
  2. Put the tomato sauce and cheese on top of the pizza dough
  3. Put it in the oven at MAXIMUM HEAT (250 Celsius) for 6-8 minutes
  4. When it's done pull it apart from the oven and put the oregano on top
  5. You're ready to enjoy!
