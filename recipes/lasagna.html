Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a typical italian dish. It is conformed with pasta, tomato sauce, ground meat and spices.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Grounded meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Put the tomato sauce and the grounded meat into a frying pan and cook it, mixing it together
  3. Layer the lasagna, one of pasta then one of meat sauce, do it until you full the bowl
  4. Put it in the oven for 50 minutes at 190 Celsius
